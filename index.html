A very warm welcome to all of you, today I will let yo know about the tasty food from Jammu&Kashmir. I will give a very short introduction about each and every dish. So lets get started :) >>

  1. Rajma Chawal – A common veg staple food

    Red kidney beans or rajma curry served with rice. The sweetness of kidney beans are exclusive to Jammu, hence the dish is quite a popular one.Spices like chili and turmeric powder along with onions and tomatoes make this main dish a delectable one. The mild aroma that it imparts is a result of using fresh coriander leaves.
    You can also have a look on the recipe of Rajma Chawal for fast cooking in this link.

  2. Dum Aloo – A typical Kashmiri food

    Aloo or potatoes cooked with spices. Since this dish was originally consumed by Kashmiri Pandits, it was initially prepared without garlic and onions.The yogurt based gravy has a rich yet subtle taste of spices like garam masala, cumin, fennel seeds, cardamom and other spices. Hot parathas go well with this preparation.
    You can also have a look on the recipe of Dum Aloo for fast cooking in thislink.

  3. Polao –A rice preparation

    An amalgamation of rice and fruits along with milk and sugar.Aromatic basmati rice perfectly mingled with seasonal fruits, tastes best when teamed up with a meat preparation.
    You can also have a look on the recipe of Kashmiri Polao for fast cooking in thislink.

  4. Khatta Meat – A Dogri food

    A mutton preparation defined by its gravy or curry. This preparation is an upshot of the Dogra dynasty that had substantial supremacy over the state.As the name suggests, khatta means sour, and it is sour but in a delicious way. The tangy flavor is achieved either by anardana (pomegranate seeds) or amchur (dry mango) powder.
    You can also have a look on the recipe of Khatta Meat for fast cooking in this link.

  5. Kachaloo Chaat - Famous street food of Jammu

    Spiced up taro roots, when prepared with potatoes or aloo, called aloo kachaloo chaat.This sumptuous snack offers a combined taste of lemon, red chili flakes, black-eyed beans, and tamarind in the best way possible. Tangy and spicy to its core, this is a must for you.
    You can also have a look on the recipe of Kachaloo Chaat for fast cooking in thislink.
